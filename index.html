Pruning

Regular, thoughtful pruning is really the secret to keeping a garden looking its best. When I take the time to make careful cuts, it not only freshens up the look but also encourages stronger, healthier growth for the long run. It turns simple upkeep into something that feels a bit like creating art.

Gardening

I love helping people create outdoor spaces that feel alive and well cared for. From thoughtful planting to keeping weeds in check and looking after the whole landscape, I’m here to make your garden feel vibrant, welcoming, and beautifully put together.

Lawn Mowing

I like to keep things simple, efficient, and easy on your budget. When it makes sense, I’m happy to use the tools and equipment you already have, which helps cut down on extra costs. That way, you get great service without the hefty price tag, and everything stays straightforward and affordable

Hi there, my name is Mary, and I’ve been tending to plants, soil, and outdoor spaces for more than 30 years. Most of that time has been spent caring for my own garden, but I’ve also run a small gardening business on and off over the years. No matter how busy life gets, I always seem to find my way back to the garden — it’s where I feel most at home.

These days, a lot of my time is devoted to looking after my ageing parents and keeping my household running smoothly. Even so, I still make space for a small circle of clients whose gardens I care for with the same attention and joy I give my own. It’s a lovely way to stay connected to the work I love while supporting myself a little along the way.

Gardening has always been more than a job for me — it’s a source of peace, creativity, and genuine happiness. If I can bring some of that warmth and life into someone else’s outdoor space, then I’m exactly where I’m meant to be.
